Welcome, [Name]. This folder is your client engine. Your profile lives in `about/`, and everything you build lands in `outputs/`. It's all yours, on your machine.

## The one rule

**Always open Claude (Cowork) in this folder.** That's how it finds your profile and saves your work in the right place. If Claude ever seems to have forgotten who you are, you're probably in the wrong folder.

## The weekly routine

Monday, one sentence: **"Build my target list for this week."**
Claude finds companies that fit your ideal client, you approve the ones worth pursuing, and it finds the right person at each.

Then, per prospect, one sentence: **"Build a value-add for [company]."**
Claude researches, drafts, and formats. You review at every step. It stops 3 times to show you its work:

1. **The research.** You confirm the facts, especially the people.
2. **The draft.** You edit, then say "format it."
3. **The message.** You hit send. Claude never sends anything.

## What lives where

- `about/` — who you are, your offer, your voice. Say "update my offer" anytime it changes.
- `outputs/company-lists/` — your weekly shortlists.
- `outputs/company-profiles/` — company profiles, one per company. These get reused, so they compound.
- `outputs/value-adds/` — the finished, branded snapshots you send.
- `outputs/messages/` — the notes that carry them.

## When something looks off

Say so in plain English. "That org chart is wrong" or "this reads too formal" both work. Claude fixes it and remembers for next time within the session. If it keeps happening, tell Katie in the community so the skill itself gets fixed for everyone.
